About Me

Hi, I’m Krishna K—a product manager with four years of experience, including 1.3 years of bootstrapping a startup as a cofounder and product owner. I’ve built D2C products from scratch, driving adoption, engagement, and revenue growth, navigating both early-stage chaos and the exhilarating 1-to-10 scale-up journey.

If you love discussing products, ideas, or anything that sparks curiosity, feel free to reach out. I’m always up for conversations that expand perspectives and create value for both sides.

I also share my thoughts in blog and quote form—bite-sized reflections borrowed from books, people, stories, and observations. I don’t claim originality, only the joy of being a grateful benefactor of great knowledge. Click the Food for Thought button above to explore.

Outside of work, you’ll find me trekking in the Himalayas, swimming, or in meditation (sometimes just staring into the abyss of my own thoughts). My spiritual curiosity often leads me to explore different religions, mostly Indian philosophies. And yes, I’m a die-hard MS Dhoni fan—because, well, who wouldn’t want a captain cool mindset in life?
