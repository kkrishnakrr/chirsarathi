Food for Thought

Welcome to Chir Sarathi!

The nature of the universe is always transitory—even in our understanding. In one way of looking at it, no two moments are the same; nothing is predictably defined. Even what we call a “moment” is itself not defined. But again, looking at the same universe in another way, it appears everything is pre-defined, every moment is certain irrespective of our wills and efforts. Time is stagnant, and everything comes down to our way of perceiving, which makes us see all changing.

Hence, the universe itself keeps transitioning in these two ways in our perspective.

Take your time, don’t stress yourself a lot, don’t be in a hurry; everything will be alright in the end.
